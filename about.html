We Are
Life Restoration Christian Center

We share the love of Christ, teach the truth of His Word, and create a community where faith grows and lives are transformed.

Our Purpose.

Our purpose is to be a lighthouse of hope and restoration in our community and beyond. We exist to: Restore broken hearts and lives through the Word of God and the love of Christ. Equip believers to grow spiritually and become effective disciples and leaders. Empower our members to serve God, one another, and the world with excellence and compassion. Impact our city and nation through outreach, evangelism, and acts of service that reflect God’s kingdom. Together, we are committed to seeing lives transformed and God’s glory revealed in every area of life.

Our Mission.

At Life Restoration Christian Center, our mission is to restore lives through the transformative power of Jesus Christ. We are committed to preaching the Gospel, teaching biblical truth, and equipping believers to live victorious, Spirit-filled lives. Through worship, prayer, discipleship, and community outreach, we aim to build a Christ-centered environment where individuals and families are healed, strengthened, and empowered to fulfill their God-given purpose.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We believe that discovering who we are in Christ is the foundation of a victorious Christian life. At Life Restoration Christian Center, we teach that every believer is a new creation, redeemed and restored by the blood of Jesus. We encourage our members to embrace their identity as sons and daughters of God, walking in confidence, freedom, and purpose. This value reminds us that our worth is not defined by the world, our past, or our mistakes, but by the love of God and the finished work of Christ on the cross.

Church Is Family

We see the church not as a building, but as a family where everyone belongs. We value authentic relationships and a culture of love, acceptance, and mutual support. Our goal is to create an environment where no one feels alone, where we rejoice with those who rejoice and stand with those who are hurting. As a family, we grow together, serve together, and carry each other’s burdens, reflecting the heart of Christ and demonstrating His love to the world.

Kingdom Culture

We are committed to building a culture that reflects heaven on earth. Kingdom culture is about living by God’s standards — a life marked by righteousness, peace, joy, and faith. We intentionally teach and model principles of God’s Kingdom in every area: relationships, finances, leadership, and service. Our desire is to influence our homes, communities, and nations by being salt and light, transforming society through the power of God’s Word and the example of Christlike living.

Spirit Led Life

We believe that the Christian life is meant to be lived in partnership with the Holy Spirit. We value prayer, worship, and spiritual sensitivity, seeking His direction in all decisions and ministries. Living a Spirit-led life means walking in obedience, being bold in faith, and allowing the gifts of the Spirit to operate freely among us. Our desire is for every believer to experience God’s presence daily and to fulfill their divine assignment with His power and guidance.


What We Believe.

The Bible is God’s Word.

We believe the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God, our ultimate authority for faith and life. It is God’s complete revelation to humanity, teaching us how to live, how to love, and how to follow Him. At Life Restoration Christian Center, we commit to preaching and teaching the Word with clarity and truth so that believers can grow in faith and be fully equipped for every good work.

Salvation Through Jesus Christ

We believe that salvation is a free gift from God, made possible only through the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Humanity was separated from God through sin, but by grace, we are reconciled to Him through faith in Jesus. We teach that every person must repent, believe, and confess Christ as Lord to receive eternal life. This salvation brings forgiveness, freedom, and a restored relationship with God.

The Power and Presence of the Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit is active and present in the life of every believer. He empowers us to live holy lives, guides us into all truth, and equips us with spiritual gifts for the work of ministry. At Life Restoration Christian Center, we encourage believers to be filled with the Spirit and to develop an intimate relationship with Him so they can live in power and boldness, fulfilling God’s plan for their lives.

The Church as the Body of Christ

We believe the Church is not just a building but a living, active body of believers who represent Christ on earth. We are called to gather in fellowship, worship together, grow in discipleship, and reach the lost with the Good News. We see the Church as a spiritual family, a training ground for leaders, and a place of restoration and empowerment where every member discovers and fulfills their God-given calling.
